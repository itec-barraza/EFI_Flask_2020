Our Story

Sarasa Games was founded in 2010 to develop, publish, and support the most player-focused games in the world.
In 2016, we released one of our major titles, Blade & Soul, following the success of our hit True-Action Combat in previous titles.

Founded by Brandon Beck and Marc Merrill, and led by CEO Nicolo Laurent.
Sarasa is headquartered in Paris, France, and has 1,200+ members in 6+ offices worldwide.



Our values

Rich game mechanics, Player Interaction, constant Support and Communication, always Innovating.
